The Big Bang Theory is a comedy about brilliant physicists, Leonard and Sheldon, who understand how the universe works. But none of that helps them interact with people, especially women. All this begins to change when a free-spirited beauty named Penny moves in next door.

2007, TV-14, 12 Seasons
